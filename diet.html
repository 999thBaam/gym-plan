Weekly Cut Diet

Every single day

Whey: 1 scoop in water, never milk — right after the workout. Chai: max 2 cups, 1 tsp sugar each. 10–15 min walk after dinner — non-negotiable. Night: 1 tsp isabgol in warm water if no motion that day.

Order of eating

Kheera/salad first → protein → sabzi → roti last. Appetite crashes? Drop roti first, protein never gets dropped.

Gut rules

Never more than ~45–50 g protein in one sitting. Curd daily. Burps persist past week 2 → cut eggs to 3, add 20 g soya to breakfast chilla.

Weigh-in

Wednesday mornings only — after toilet, before food. Post-cheat scale (Sun–Mon) lies by 1–2 kg of water. Log it below ↓

All foods — tap for nutrition + GI

GI badge: LOW ≤55 MED 56–69 HIGH 70+ GI — (no carbs). Low GI = steady energy, better fat loss.

Limit or avoid

  • GI 78 Aloo — a few cubes mixed in sabzi fine; aloo-as-the-sabzi, no.
  • GI 73 White rice — if you must: ½ katori with dal, once a week, never with chicken curry (roti instead).
  • ⚠ Sugary drinks, juice, sweet lassi — fastest way to erase a week's deficit.
  • ⚠ Refined-oil deep-fried anything — ghee is stable at high heat and more satiating, but it's 45 kcal/tsp: the cap matters more than the choice.
